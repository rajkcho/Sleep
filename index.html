Frequently Asked Questions

Is InvoiceFlow really free?

Yes! The core features are 100% free forever. You can create up to 3 invoices per month with a small watermark. Pro removes the watermark and adds unlimited invoices.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Your invoice data is stored locally in your browser. Just fill in the details and download your PDF instantly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your data stays in your browser. We never send your invoice information to any server. Your drafts are saved locally.

Can I add my company logo?

Yes! Just click the logo upload area and select your company logo. It will appear on your invoice instantly.

What file formats are supported?

Invoices are downloaded as high-quality PDF files, ready to send to your clients or print.

Can I send invoices directly?

Click the "Email" button to open your default email client with a pre-filled message. Just attach the PDF and send!
